Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Claudionoid account and all associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to support@claudionoid.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your Claudionoid account
  3. We will confirm your identity and process the deletion within 30 days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email when your account has been deleted
Send Deletion Request →
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to proceed before sending the request.

What data is deleted

  • ✕ Deleted Your account (name, email, password)
  • ✕ Deleted Subscription and billing history
  • ✕ Deleted Usage data and token history
  • ✕ Deleted Any stored preferences or settings
  • ↻ Auto-deleted Conversation history (stored locally on your device — clear manually in app Settings)

Note: Stripe may retain billing records for up to 7 years as required by financial regulations. This data is held by Stripe, not by Claudionoid.

Cancel your subscription first

If you have an active subscription, please cancel it before requesting account deletion to avoid future charges.

You can cancel your subscription inside the app under Settings → Plan → Cancel, or by contacting us.
